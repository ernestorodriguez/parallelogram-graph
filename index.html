Draw Parallelogram

This's a simple App to draw a parallelogram and show you the area for the shape you drew.

To start, click on the screen, by repeating it this, you can select three points for the parallelogram. Pressing the mouse button over a point and holding it, you can move the point around, this will modify the current shape, and show you the new calculated area.

You can start all over, making click on the "restart" button.
